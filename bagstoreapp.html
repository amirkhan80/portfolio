Software Name: GymStore Android Application GymStore is a simple Android shopping application developed for gym and fitness products. The app allows users to log in, view gym products, add items to the cart, manage product quantity, and make payments using different payment methods. The application is designed with a user-friendly interface so users can easily shop for fitness equipment and accessories. The app contains different modules such as Login, Registration, Product Store, Cart, Payment, Orders, and Profile Management. Users can increase or decrease product quantity, view the total price, and place orders successfully. The application also includes payment options like UPI and Cash on Delivery (COD).
